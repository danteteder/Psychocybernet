## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Email Deliverability Collapse
**Scenario**: Domains get blacklisted, reply rates drop to 1%

**Mitigation**:
- Warmup: Strict 30-day ramp per account
- Rotation: 4 domains, spread sends evenly
- Content: A/B test 5+ variations, kill low performers
- Backup: Buy 20 backup domains (ready to warm)
- Diversify: LinkedIn, cold calls, paid ads (20% of leads)

### Risk 2: Sales Rep Churn
**Scenario**: Reps quit after 2 weeks (no closes)

**Mitigation**:
- Onboarding: Set expectations (4-6 weeks to first close)
- Motivation: Leaderboard, weekly wins, public recognition
- Support: Daily huddles, objection handling scripts
- Replacement: Always recruiting (pipeline of 50+ candidates)
- Automation: AI handles 50% of early funnel (less rep dependency)

### Risk 3: Market Saturation
**Scenario**: Estonia exhausted, expansion markets underperform

**Mitigation**:
- Vertical depth: Go deeper, not wider (more offers per industry)
- Product pivot: SaaS + education (recurring revenue)
- Geographic arbitrage: Eastern Europe, Asia, LatAm (untapped)
- Acquisition: Buy competitors' lead lists or agencies

### Risk 4: Delivery Bottleneck
**Scenario**: Too many closes, can't fulfill (reputation damage)

**Mitigation**:
- Capacity caps: Max 20 new clients/month until team scaled
- White-label: Partner agencies for overflow
- Hiring: Pre-vetted freelancer bench (Upwork, Toptal)
- Productization: Templates reduce custom work 50%

### Risk 5: Cash Flow Crunch
**Scenario**: Clients pay net-30/60, commissions due now

**Mitigation**:
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery (standard)
- Factoring: Invoice financing for enterprise deals
- Reserve: 3 months operating expenses in business account
- Commission timing: Pay on client payment, not close
