### Yellow (Adjustments Needed)
- ⚠️ Reply rate <4% (rewrite sequences)

- ⚠️ Domain health <90% (reduce volume, re-warm)
- ⚠️ <3 reps active (re-recruit, simplify onboarding)
- ⚠️ No calls booked (improve reply → call CTA)

### Red (Pause & Fix)
- 🛑 Domains blacklisted (stop sending, warm new domains)
- 🛑 0 replies after 1,000 emails (sequence is broken)
- 🛑 Reps quitting immediately (onboarding problem)
- 🛑 Technical failures (webhooks, dashboard, CRM)

**If Red**: Stop, diagnose, fix within 24 hours, restart.
